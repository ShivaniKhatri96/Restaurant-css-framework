Starlight

The place to be

Enjoy the pleasant flavor under the night sky with the meals that remind you of home

Starlight was lauched 10 years ago with the hopes of making evening meals a delight specially to people with busy schedules. Within the first five years, we managed to garner a huge success which encouraged us to lauch at two new locations: Kessel-Lo and Herent. So far, Starlight family has provided us with love and support and we are confident that this love will last a long time.
